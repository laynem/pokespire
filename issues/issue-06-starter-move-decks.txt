ISSUE #6 — Starter Move Decks
Type: AFK | Blocked by: Issue #5

WHAT TO BUILD
Wire all real Gen 1 moves for the 4 starters into the combat engine with accurate
power values, energy costs, and effects. Each starter begins with 4 moves as their deck.

Move card design (each card shows):
  - Move name
  - Type badge (colour coded)
  - Energy cost (1-3)
  - Damage / effect description
  - PP counter (e.g. 3/3)

Starter starting decks:

Bulbasaur:
  - Tackle      | Normal | 1 energy | 40 power
  - Growl       | Normal | 1 energy | Lower enemy Attack 1 stage
  - Vine Whip   | Grass  | 2 energy | 45 power
  - Leech Seed  | Grass  | 2 energy | Drain 10 HP/turn from enemy

Charmander:
  - Scratch     | Normal | 1 energy | 40 power
  - Growl       | Normal | 1 energy | Lower enemy Attack 1 stage
  - Ember       | Fire   | 2 energy | 40 power, 10% chance Burn
  - Smokescreen | Normal | 1 energy | 30% miss chance applied to enemy next turn

Squirtle:
  - Tackle      | Normal | 1 energy | 40 power
  - Tail Whip   | Normal | 1 energy | Lower enemy Defense 1 stage
  - Water Gun   | Water  | 2 energy | 40 power
  - Withdraw    | Water  | 1 energy | Gain 8 Block this turn

Pikachu:
  - Thundershock | Electric | 2 energy | 40 power, 10% chance Paralysis
  - Growl        | Normal   | 1 energy | Lower enemy Attack 1 stage
  - Tail Whip    | Normal   | 1 energy | Lower enemy Defense 1 stage
  - Quick Attack | Normal   | 1 energy | 40 power, always goes first this turn

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
[ ] All 4 starter decks initialised correctly in runStore on game start
[ ] Move cards render with name, type, energy cost, power/effect, and PP counter
[ ] All move effects implemented: damage, stat changes, status, block, priority
[ ] Type badge colours match official Pokemon type colours
[ ] Quick Attack resolves before enemy action regardless of speed
[ ] Stat stage modifiers affect damage calculation correctly
