ISSUE #7 — Post-Combat Rewards & Move Draft
Type: AFK | Blocked by: Issue #6

WHAT TO BUILD
After winning any combat, show a reward screen. Player earns gold and is offered
a move draft: pick 1 of 3 moves to add to the active Pokemon's deck, or skip.

Reward screen shows:
  - Gold earned (wild battle: 10-20g, trainer battle: 25-40g)
  - Move draft: 3 move cards drawn from the active Pokemon's full Gen 1 learnset
      Each card shows: name, type, energy cost, power/effect
  - "Skip" option to take gold only and continue

Rules:
  - Moves already in the Pokemon's deck are excluded from the draft pool
  - If all learnable moves are already in deck, skip draft and award bonus gold
  - Draft is always for the Pokemon that finished the combat (the active one)
  - Party screen accessible from this screen to review full decks

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
[ ] Reward screen appears after every combat win
[ ] Correct gold amount added to runStore
[ ] 3 move cards shown, from Pokemon's Gen 1 learnset, excluding duplicates
[ ] Selecting a move adds it to that Pokemon's move deck in runStore
[ ] Skip option bypasses move addition
[ ] Bonus gold awarded if no new moves available
[ ] "Continue" button returns player to map
