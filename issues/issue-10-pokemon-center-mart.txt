ISSUE #10 — Pokemon Center & Poke Mart
Type: AFK | Blocked by: Issue #8, Issue #9

WHAT TO BUILD
Two rest/shop node types on the map.

--- POKEMON CENTER ---
Options (player picks one):
  A) Heal All    — restore all party Pokemon to full HP and cure all status
  B) Move Tutor  — pick a Pokemon, forget one of its current moves,
                   learn a new one from its Gen 1 learnset

--- POKE MART ---
Randomised shop inventory (4-6 items per visit):
  - Passive items from the items pool (Issue #9)  — 75-150g each
  - Move Scroll: buy a specific move for a Pokemon  — 50-100g
  - Potion: restore 40 HP to one Pokemon            — 40g
  - Full Restore: full HP + cure status on one Pokemon — 120g
  - Poke Ball: improves the Pokemon offered on next Catch node — 60g

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
[ ] Pokemon Center offers Heal All and Move Tutor as distinct options
[ ] Heal All restores all party Pokemon to maxHp and clears status
[ ] Move Tutor lets player pick a Pokemon, then swap one move for a learnset move
[ ] Poke Mart renders randomised shop inventory with gold costs
[ ] Buying an item deducts gold and adds item to runStore
[ ] Buying a Move Scroll adds the move to the chosen Pokemon's deck
[ ] Gold balance shown and updates in real time
[ ] Purchase buttons disabled when insufficient gold
[ ] Continue button returns to map
