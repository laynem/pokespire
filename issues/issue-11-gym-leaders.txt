ISSUE #11 — Gym Leaders & Boss Encounters
Type: AFK | Blocked by: Issue #8

WHAT TO BUILD
Three scripted boss encounters, one closing each act. Each Gym Leader has a themed
team as close to their original Gen 1 lineup as possible. Winning grants a Gym Badge
with a passive run bonus and a guaranteed item reward.

--- BROCK (Act 1 Boss) — Boulder Badge ---
Party:
  - Geodude  Lv12 | Rock/Ground | Tackle, Defense Curl, Rock Throw
  - Onix     Lv14 | Rock/Ground | Tackle, Screech, Rock Throw, Bind
AI pattern: Leads with Geodude. Switches to Onix when Geodude faints.
            Prioritises Screech to lower player Defense before attacking.
Badge bonus: All party Pokemon gain +10% Defense permanently

--- MISTY (Act 2 Boss) — Cascade Badge ---
Party:
  - Staryu   Lv18 | Water        | Water Gun, Rapid Spin, Harden
  - Starmie  Lv21 | Water/Psychic| Water Gun, Psychic, Swift, Recover
AI pattern: Leads with Staryu. Starmie uses Recover when below 40% HP.
Badge bonus: All Water-type moves deal +15% damage

--- LT. SURGE (Act 3 Boss) — Thunder Badge ---
Party:
  - Voltorb  Lv21 | Electric | Thundershock, Screech, Sonic Boom
  - Pikachu  Lv18 | Electric | Thunderbolt, Quick Attack, Thunder Wave
  - Raichu   Lv24 | Electric | Thunderbolt, Mega Punch, Thunder Wave, Slam
AI pattern: Leads with Voltorb. Prioritises Thunder Wave to paralyse.
            Raichu sweeps once player is debilitated.
Badge bonus: All party Pokemon gain +1 energy on the first turn of each combat

Rewards (all bosses):
  - Large gold reward (150-200g)
  - Choice of 1 of 2 rare items
  - Gym Badge added to run state

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
[ ] Each Gym Leader has correct scripted Pokemon team with accurate Gen 1 moves
[ ] Enemy AI follows described patterns (switching, healing, status priority)
[ ] Badge awarded and stored in runStore on win
[ ] Badge passive bonus applies correctly for the rest of the run
[ ] Boss encounter has distinct visual treatment (gym arena background)
[ ] Post-boss reward screen shows gold + item choice
[ ] Defeating Lt. Surge triggers the victory screen
